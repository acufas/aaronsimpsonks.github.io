Aaron Simpson

Short Bio

Aaron Simpson is a software engineer at TreviPay, a B2B commerce fintech company. Previously, he worked in healthcare technology at Cerner. His interests include mathematics, computer science, biology/medicine, and economics. He eagerly welcomes email or DMs on Twitter.

Long Bio

Born and raised in Kansas City, KS, Aaron received his B.Sc in Computer Science from Kansas State University. As a child, he enjoyed reading books and researching topics like engineering, science, and astronomy. After college, he spent a short time at healthcare IT giant Cerner before moving on to TreviPay. Still, his heart is in medicine and healthtech. Other interests include business, mathematics, computer science, and economics.

The internet can be a valuable tool for networking and meeting interesting people. Because of this, Aaron eagerly welcomes messages on Twitter (@aaronsimpsonks) or through email (aaron@aaronsimpson.org).
